Crossbar I Fonts

Many sans serif typefaces don’t differentiate between the uppercase “I” and the lowercase “l” very well. Helvetica, for example, a classic and well-known typeface, doesn’t incorporporate very much distinction between those characters. This a legibility problem.

The typefaces featured on this site have an easily-seen distinction between the uppercase “I” and the lowercase “l”.
